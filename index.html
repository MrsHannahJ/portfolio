CBTTF Records

Responsive Wordpress Site with Customized Theme

This client had an existing site built with the builder provided by 000webhosting. They wanted to retain the free hosting service, which created a few challenges. The free hosting has limited bandwidth and security, so it has sluggish performance and cannot support e-commerce due to security concerns. But, we were able to create some work-arounds and were still able to attain the "customized, dark, art deco" feel that they desired.

I designed and custom built a site that better supported product sales and user experience. I also setup a customized Wordpress admin so that the not-so-tech-friendly user would be able to maintain the site into the future without needing to hire a developer.

About Me

I am a self-taught front-end web developer, particularly interested in working with small businesses and non-profits. As a small business owner and community volunteer, I have a passion to help grow others working within these communities.

I wear many hats: business owner, freelancer, artist, teacher, scientist, web developer.

I'm a research junkie who obsesses over my code. And I can Google most people under the table. What sets me apart most is my integrity and my genuine care for my clients. I will tell you if I don't know how to do something; I will advise you if I think there is a more affordable solution to your problem.

I will work within the specific wants, needs and budget to find the best option for each of my clients. Though I have a preferred workflow and don't necessarily care for bloated CMSs or frameworks, I understand that it is often what makes the most sense for my client. I wouldn't call myself a designer, but I do have a basic grasp of user-friendly design.
